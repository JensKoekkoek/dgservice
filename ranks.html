Ranks

Below is the list of ranks within the Friends Chat. If you would like a rank with us, please read our Rules before clicking “Apply”.

We also have an in depth Dungeoneering Guide for those who are wanting to learn/improve in the skill.

RECRUIT / RUSH

Rush ranks can sell C1 rushes, boss item hunts and all craftable items (given they have the level requirement). Rush ranks can sell up to 4 non-afk leeches at a time but only 1 afk leech at a time.

Requirements:

  • A small quiz completed on application - trial is not needed
  • 40 magic
  • 57 Dungeoneering
  • Cosmic runes and a tier 40 staff bound
  • 10m deposit
  • Having Daemonheim Aura 2 is highly recommended

CORPORAL / MED

Med ranks can sell Medium floors to up to 4 leeches at a time, C6 smalls AND those of a Recruit rank.

Requirements:

  • 2000 total level
  • Daemonheim Aura 2
  • 5m deposit
  • Must pass a med trial

SERGEANT / 1S1L

1s1l ranks can sell large floors, hard mode AND anything of a Corporal/Med rank and below.

They can only take 1 leech at a time. They are also allowed to 2s2l with other 1s1l ranks or above. A 1s1l rank may not sell 2 large leeches, (1s2l), except with the permission from an Admin on a floor by floor basis.

Requirements:

  • 2400 total level WITHOUT Invention
  • 101 Dungeoneering
  • Daemonheim Aura 4
  • Recommended completion of Warped Gorajan Trailblazer outfit
  • Recommended 70 Invention for lock melters
  • Must pass a 1s1l trial

LIEUTENANT / 2S3L

2s3l ranks can take up to 3 leeches at a time WITH a duo partner (DP). Any ranks within the FC may offer to be the DP, which the 2s3l can choose to decline.

Requirements:

  • 2500 total level WITHOUT Invention
  • 110 Dungeoneering
  • Ancient Curses
  • Daemonheim Aura 4
  • Recommended completion of Warped Gorajan Trailblazer outfit
  • Recommended 70 Invention for lock melters
  • Must pass a 2s3l trial
